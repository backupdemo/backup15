About Us

The pioneering leader of the general cyber-security trainings. Trainings are the best way to empower employees as humans are the weakest link in any security system. Various organizations provide general cyber-security training yet major data breaches are encountered almost every month. Traditionally, such trainings consist of videos educating users along with questionnaires related to the topics. However, this approach lacks practical experience and the participants lose the will to see the course through to completion in most cases.

Backup is different.

We at backup have carefully identified essential cyber threats everyone should be aware of in 2019. These threats are simple yet effective which targets humans to exploit organizations, even the state of the art 2-factor authentication system is powerless against such attacks.

We use effective techniques like experience-based learning which gives the user a hands-on experience of how these attacks could be executed and would lure them into falling into the trap. The multiplayer feature is especially designed to make the users face phishing attacks crafted by other users and our experts to give them an experience in handling such attacks. We even provide continuous training . Each week we send 2-3 general cyber attack related situations that the user needs to clear to gain points which improves their score in performance reports.

At the end of our training we provide a detailed report containing information about how the user performed and possible scopes for further improvement.
